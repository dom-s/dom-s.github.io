About Me

Summary

Hi! I am an applied researcher at eBay, where I am part of the Search Ranking and Monetization Team. Previously, I received a Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I was part of the Text Information Management and Analysis Group, and was advised by Prof. ChengXiang Zhai.

My general research interests are at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Information Retrieval, Machine Learning and Privacy/Security. My research focuses on modeling and understanding user behavior on social media and e-commerce, especially related to textual user data in a search context. As part of my research journey I have published work in text classification and retrieval, domain-adaptation, personalization, information extraction and knowledge bases.
